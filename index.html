About Braids & Beauty

Passionate about African braiding art for over 10 years, I put all my know-how at the service of your beauty. Each hairstyle is made with care and precision, using traditional African techniques adapted to modern needs.

Specialized in African braids, I welcome you in a warm space where relaxation and elegance meet. Whether you choose knotless braids, french curls or fulani braids, each creation is unique and adapted to your style.
